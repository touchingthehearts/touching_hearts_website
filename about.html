About Us

At Touching the Hearts of Children Daycare, our mission is to provide a nurturing, safe, and fun environment where your child feels secure, respected, and loved.

We focus on developing social, emotional, language, and motor skills through play, music, stories, sensory exploration, and outdoor time. We aim to make a difference in your child’s heart every day.
